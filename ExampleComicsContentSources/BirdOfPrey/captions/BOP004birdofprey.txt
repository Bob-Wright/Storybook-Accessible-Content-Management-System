Finally an armed government and a few private armies substituted for the former rule of law. The plutocratic oligarchy which kept these forces &#34;on retainer&#34; assumed an interest in limiting the success of those who left the enclaves. No one used the word &#34;escaped&#34;, it was always &#34;left&#34;. It almost made it seem as though the &#34;resident population&#34; was free to depart at any time.
